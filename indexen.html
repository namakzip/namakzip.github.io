Our Specials

돼지곰탕(Pig Gom Tang)

A menu item featuring thinly sliced, low-temperature aged pork shoulder, cooked and served on a signature broth. The broth is a blend of vegetable stock, enhanced with kelp and anchovies, and pork bone broth.

고기칼국수(Meat kal guk su)

A menu item featuring traditional Korean noodles (Kalguksu) instead of rice in pork bone soup. The dish includes thinly sliced, low-temperature aged pork shoulder, cooked and served on top.

수육(su yuk)

A menu that boils various parts of high-quality pigs every day on the principle of exhaustion

Michelin Guide Opinion

As a city known for its pork soup, Busan offers a variety of styles in its soup dishes. While maintaining the unique flavor of pork soup, each restaurant has its own method of preparing the broth, adding to the diversity. At Na Mak Zip, the broth is light and clean, with a higher ratio of clear broth to meat broth, providing a refreshing taste. In addition to the signature pork soup, customers can also enjoy dishes like meat kalguksu and knife-cut noodles made with the same broth, as well as boiled pork slices and grilled pork belly. With its modern atmosphere, Na Mak Zip offers the perfect setting to enjoy a satisfying pork soup with a clean finish
